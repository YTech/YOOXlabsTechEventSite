Technology Event Front-end Matters

Wednesday, November 18th, 2015

FRONT-END MATTERS is an event hosted by YOOXlabs dedicated to professionals who love the web, love learning and want to get to know all aspects of front-end development. The aim of the meetup is to keep front-end developers who want to always be up to date on technologies and tools to optimize their work-flow.

That is why we are opening our doors once again for the second tech show, which will feature some of the latest technologies and tools available in front-end development.
