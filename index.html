Technology Event Front-end Matters

Wednesday, November 18th, 2015

Know-how, passion for technology and appetite for innovation permeate YOOXlabs and live in its developers. Technological platform for the YOOX Net-a-Porter Group, specifically designed for online fashion, YOOXlabs has been the catalyst for acceleration and innovation, making the fusion between eCommerce and luxury real.

YOOXlabs is now the heart of our innovation, leader in the engineering of a constant improvement in user experience and website performance through the application of best practices and latest web technologies. That's why we're opening the doors again for our second technological show, which will feature some of the latest technologies and tools available in front-end development.

The event will feature 1 talk from our guest Michele Carrì, an introduction to the Ember.js framework, plus 4 talks from YOOXlabs developers about Angular.js, developing an atypical theme for WordPress, a tool to improve your workflow with SVG and another to automate the testing process of your website or web application.
